**Step 1: OCR Transcription**
First, transcribe ALL text you can read from the image exactly as you see it. Present this as a single block of text.

**Step 2: Analysis and JSON Extraction**
Based on the text you transcribed in Step 1, analyze it and fill in the following JSON object.

**JSON Schema:**
{
  "merchant_name": "string or null",
  "total_amount": "integer or null",
  "bill_date": "string in YYYY-MM-DD format or null",
  "category_name": "string from the list",
  "category_type":" 1 or 0"
  "note": "string",
}

**Rules for Extraction:**
-   **`bill_date`**: Must be formatted as **YYYY-MM-DD**. Assume the current year is 2025.
-   **`category_name`**: Choose the most fitting category from the list below.
-   **Missing/Unreadable Info**: Use `null` for any field that cannot be determined."merchant_name" will be fill as "Payment"
-   **"note": Some short details of transaction. Maybe list some things that bought in the bill
-   **"category_type": Must be 1 or 0. 1 if it is Thu nhập type and 0 if it is Chi tiêu type
**List of Categories:**
* **Chi tiêu:** Ăn uống, Xe cộ, Mua sắm, Học tập, Đầu tư, Y tế, Du lịch, Điện, Nước, Mạng Internet, Thuê Nhà, Giải trí, Thú cưng, Dịch vụ, Sửa chữa, Quà tặng, Chi tiêu khác.
* **Thu nhập:** Lương, Tiền lãi đầu tư, Tiền cho thuê nhà, Thu nhập khác.
